How Foundation Works

A clear workflow map for non-technical business owners.

1. CaptureForms, email, WhatsApp, Facebook, ads, or direct API webhooks enter one intake layer.
2. EnrichCompany data, message intent, source, history, and urgency are structured for scoring.
3. ScoreAI classifies hot, warm, low priority, or spam using your business criteria.
4. RouteHot leads alert Slack or SMS. Warm leads enter nurture. Everything logs to CRM.
5. AuditEvery decision is recorded so response time, accuracy, and drift can be reviewed.
